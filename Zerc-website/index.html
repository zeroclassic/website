What is ZeroClassic?

ZeroClassic is a revolutionary cryptocurrency and transaction platform based on Zcash.

ZeroClassic is a fork of ZeroCurrency and ZERO. ZERO is a fork of Bitcoin that adds shielded transaction via zk-SNARKs. When ZERO developers decided to fork, changing the consensus rules by adding a developer fee and coin cap, the chain continued under the old rules as ZeroClassic.

FAQ

Frequently asked questions

1 How do I claim my coins from ZERO?

Simply install the ZeroClassic wallet and import your ZERO private key. Once the wallet has fully synced, all funds available at the fork height of 412300 will be available as ZERC.

2 How do I mine ZeroClassic?

See Pools for a list of available mining pools.

3 When will new exchanges be added?

Exchange listings can be very costly. Consider reaching out to an exchange you would like to have ZeroClassic listed on to inquire about listing fees. You may also suggest it through Discord where someone from the team can submit the request for you.

Please keep in mind, we rely on community support and donations for building funds towards exchange listings.

4 Where can I get further support?

If you need help, join our Discord and someone will be happy to assist you.

5 How can I help/support ZeroClassic?

Zero Classic runs on community support and donations. Consider donating or mining to our ZeroClassic Address

If you are a developer, consider submitting a PR to our GitHub
